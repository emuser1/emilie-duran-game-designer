About

A Beary Arctic Adventure is an augmented reality (AR) mobile app for children ages 6-8 offering playful educational activities, targeted toward fine arts, motor skills, and science. This Arctic-themed world featuring Beary the Polar Bear encourages interest in nature and the environment. I had the pleasure of working in a team of three, with two other students. Read more about the entire process here.

Production Contributions

  • Curated music and SFX for the app and different activities
  • Brainstormed educational areas of focus: motor skills, fine arts, and science
  • Troubleshooted any existing errors in C# scripts
  • Provided tutorials and assistance with coding

Level Design Contributions
