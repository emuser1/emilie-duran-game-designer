About

Dark Patterns is an award-winning top-down 2D dungeon crawler, with a series of short levels bringing awareness to dark patterns on the internet. Each level focuses on a specific dark pattern (i.e. pop-up ads, roach motels, privacy zuckering) to help players understand and identify them. I worked in a team of four students for my Designing Games for Impact class. Dark Patterns won first place in the games category at UM's annual Interative Media Showcase.
