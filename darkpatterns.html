About

Dark Patterns is a top-down 2D dungeon crawler, with a series of short levels bringing awareness to dark patterns on the internet. Each level focuses on a specific dark pattern (i.e. pop-up ads, roach motels, privacy zuckering) to help players understand and identify them. I joined at the game's conception, helping recruit other team members and framing its design.
