Emilie Duran

I'm a UI/UX and game designer based in South Florida. My love for everything play and accessibility strengthen my desire to make games more accessible. I'm open to new opportunities anywhere.

Currently, I'm finishing up my B.S.C. in Interactive Media with a minor Game Design at the University of Miami. As a queer second-generation immigrant that did not see themselves represented in media, I'm excited to create safe spaces for marginalized people within the world of gaming.

A few of my other interests include plant-based cooking, anime, K-pop, and mechanical keyboards.

Some of the games I'm currently playing: Genshin Impact, Omori, and Turnip Boy Commits Tax Evasion.
