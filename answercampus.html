About

Answer Campus is a visual novel, with a series of short interactive stories in a college setting as experienced by people of color. Each story focuses on a specific lens of racism and intersectionality (microaggressions, cultural taboos, implicit bias, etc.) to provide a broad picture of racism as experienced by young adults. I joined at the game's conception, helping recruit other team members and framing its design.
